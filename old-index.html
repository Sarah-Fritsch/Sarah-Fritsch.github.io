RobinAugust

RobinAugust is a 18 year old songwriter from Nashville, TN who has been writing songs for as long as she can remember. Her early inspirations came from a healthy dose of Lennon/ McCartney, and have evolved to include Radiohead's Thom Yorke, Pink Floyd's Roger Waters and Syd Barrett, and YouTube sensation's Dodie Clark. She strives to embody the recording and performance style of Freddie Mercury, Van Halen, Led Zepplin, Janis Joplin and The Runaways.

RobinAugust formed a Runaways inspired band Queens of Noise in 2016 with 3 fellow (then) 13 year old friends, and a (then) 11 year old drummer. They have played at many Nashville venues, including the highly rated Tomato Arts Festival which takes place in the heart of Nashville's alternative music scene. Queens of Noise was featured in The East Nashvillian's Women in Rock 2017 issue. http://theeastnashvillian.com/article/queens-of-noise
